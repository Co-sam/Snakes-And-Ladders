A Game Of Snakes And Ladders

This game initializes the same rules as a famous board game called Snakes and Ladders. Two players takes turn rolling the dice. The number in the dice decides the position of each player. You have to move a certain number of squares which is determined by the dice but since this is a python-version snakes and ladders, my program will calculate your position and keep you informed. If you land on the bottom of the ladder you get to reach the top of it and if you land on top of a snake's head you have to go down. Each turn of the dice comes with a new surprise and twist. Enjoy the game.

Features
-> a game of two players.
-> Random dice rolls.
-> Snakes that bring players down.
-> Ladders that take players up.
-> Tracks player positions and declares the winner.

How to Play
-> Each players take their turns to roll a dice.
-> Each player must move the exact number of numbers the dice show.
-> If you land in the bottom of a ladder, then you climb up to the top of it.
-> If you land on the top of a snake's head you slide down till the end of it's tail.
-> The first player to reach the 100th square wins.
